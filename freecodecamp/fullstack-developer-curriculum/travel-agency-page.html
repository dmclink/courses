Top Itineraries

Package 1: 3-day trip. Perfect for a holiday weekend getaway to see the city. During this bite sized tour you'll shop at local night markets in Taipei City, visit the iconic Taipei 101, the national palace museum, and take a half day tour to Yehliu Geopark.

Package 2: 7-day trip. With more time on this journey we visit all the same locations in package. We also take a coach down the coast to visit the laid-back city of Hualien. We'll go snorkeling, stand-up-paddle, and take an easy hike through the stunning Taroko National Park.
