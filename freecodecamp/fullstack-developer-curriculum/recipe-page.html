Black Pepper Lion's Mane Rice Ball

Inspired by a thing I ate at 7-11. We veganized this recipe by replacing the chicken with Lion's Mane mushroom to get the meaty texture.

Ingredients

  • Lion's mane mushrooms
  • Onion
  • Black pepper
  • Thick soy sauce
  • Sushi rice
  • Nori sheets

Instructions

  1. Mix together black pepper and soy sauce to make your sauce
  2. Marinate the lion's mane mushroom in the sauce overnight
  3. Put your rice in the rice cooker
  4. Pan fry on medium-low heat
  5. After 3 minutes flip the msuhroom
  6. Lay out the nori sheet and spread the rice in a thin layer
  7. Lay the cooked mushroom chunks in a straight line on one side of the rice
  8. Tightly roll the sheet and serve
